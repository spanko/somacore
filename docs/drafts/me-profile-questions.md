# DRAFT — `/me/profile` question wording (for Tai's review)

**Status.** DRAFT prepared 2026-07-01. This is the complete set of questions the profile page asks — the input surface for personalized targets (protein first). Tai owns the wording, especially the cycle questions. The page takes ~90 seconds; there is deliberately no BMR calculator, injury history, or dietary questionnaire.

Every question is optional. Skipping any of them means the coach keeps using population ranges for whatever that question would have informed — stated on the page so skipping feels safe, not punished.

---

## Page intro copy (proposed)

> A few questions so your targets can be *yours* instead of population averages. Everything here is optional — skip anything, change anything later. The more you fill in, the more specific your daily plan gets.

## Q1 — Body weight

> **Current weight**
> `[___] lb`
> *Have an iPhone with the SomaCore app? We can keep this current from Apple Health automatically instead.*

## Q2 — Goal

> **What are you working toward right now?**
> - ○ Recomposition — lose fat while holding or building muscle
> - ○ Cutting — losing weight is the priority
> - ○ Maintaining — happy where I am
> - ○ Gaining — building muscle and adding weight
> - ○ Prefer not to say

## Q3 — Where you are in it (shown only if Q2 ≠ Maintain / Prefer not to say)

> **Roughly where are you in that goal?** *(optional — helps the plan respect where you are, e.g. week 10 of a cut is not week 2)*
> Started: `[date]`    Weekly target: `[___] lb/week`

## Q4 — Cycle status

> **Do you have a menstrual cycle?** *(your daily targets — protein especially — shift with cycle phase; this lets the plan shift with you)*
> - ○ Yes
> - ○ I'm in perimenopause
> - ○ I'm post-menopausal
> - ○ Not applicable
> - ○ Prefer not to say

## Q5 — Cycle phase (shown only if Q4 = Yes)

> **Where are you in your cycle right now?**
> - ○ Menstrual (bleeding)
> - ○ Follicular (after bleeding, before ovulation)
> - ○ Around ovulation
> - ○ Luteal (after ovulation, before next period)
> - ○ Not sure / not tracking
> - ○ Read it from Apple Health *(iPhone app required — if you track your cycle in any app that shares with Apple Health, we can follow it automatically and you never answer this question again)*

## Wording questions for Tai

1. **Q2 option labels** — "Cutting"/"Gaining" vs. softer framing? The persona is action-first; these labels are gym-native but might not be every future user's vocabulary.
2. **Q4 lead-in** — is the parenthetical the right amount of "why we're asking"? Too clinical? Not enough?
3. **"Prefer not to say" vs. silent skip** — all questions are skippable anyway; do the explicit opt-out options earn their place (signal: "this is a normal thing to decline") or add clutter?
4. **Q5 phase descriptions** — right level of explanation for someone who doesn't track?
5. Anything here that shouldn't be asked at all in v1?

## What the coach says when the profile is empty (proposed, for consistency review)

> *Protein target today: 0.7–1 g per lb — a general range. Tell me your goal at `/me/profile` and I'll give you a number instead.*

Shown at most once a week, never on consecutive days.
